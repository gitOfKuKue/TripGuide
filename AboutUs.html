About Us

At Pathfinder , our goal is to transform travel into a sustainable and fulfilling experience for Gen Z. We aim to provide eco-friendly options that make it easy for travelers to explore responsibly—without compromising on quality or convenience. By offering green accommodations, e-bike rentals, and sustainable travel essentials, we’re building a community of travelers who care about the planet. Together, let’s make every journey a step toward a greener world.
